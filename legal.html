Terms of Engagement

II

Force Majeure & Liability

theindiaproject.world coordinates independent third-party suppliers — transport, accommodation, permits, aviation and rail. While every leg is audited six times, theindiaproject.world is not liable for acts of God, weather, seismic or road events, acts of government, permit withdrawal, strikes, or the negligence or default of any third-party supplier. Where a leg fails, a pre-identified contingency is deployed at no additional coordination fee; third-party costs remain the client's responsibility. Total liability is limited to the coordination fee received.
