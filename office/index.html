Manual

How the desk runs — and keeps running.

What this console is

A single, self-contained back office. It holds your commissions pipeline, a template library, a multilingual drafts composer, your digital visiting card, the proprietary network & portfolio database, a ledger, and the legal vault. Nothing leaves this file; when you host it, your entries persist in the browser on your machine.

How to get going — five minutes

  • Commissions: add each live enquiry, set its Front and Stage. This is the heartbeat.
  • Templates: open a memo, fill the coordinates, generate, copy into your channel of choice.
  • Network: load your trusted transporters and hotels, tag each to a portfolio and a corridor — this is the asset no competitor holds.
  • Ledger: log the value and the costs of each commission; the margin computes itself.
  • Visiting card: set your details, export the vCard, send it where a card is warranted.

The cadence that keeps it sustaining

Daily
  • Clear Today's priorities, revenue first
  • Acknowledge every new enquiry within the hour
  • Advance one commission a stage
Weekly
  • Send two Trojan-Horse memos (Front 01)
  • Open one trade file (Front 02)
  • Reconcile the ledger; confirm margin
Monthly
  • Verify five network assets afresh
  • Review portfolio balance & tiering
  • Publish one Journal field note

Persistence & privacy

Host this file in a password-protected directory (Hostinger → Files → Password Protect Directories) — never on the public site. Your data is stored only in your own browser; clearing site data clears it. Export important memos and ledgers as you go.
